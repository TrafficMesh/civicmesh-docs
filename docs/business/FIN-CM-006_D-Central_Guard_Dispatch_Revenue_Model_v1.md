# 3. Private Investigation Integration


| What this is | D-Central Guard Cooperative members who also hold private investigator licences (PSIA dual licence) can receive referrals for follow-up investigation work from D-Central Forensics. |
|---|---|
| Revenue model | PI referral fee: $0 to Guard Cooperative (relationship-based). PI investigation fees: $75–$150/hour billed directly by PI to client (insurance company, HOA, municipality). D-Central Forensics earns an evidence package preparation fee separately. |
| Why it matters | Many enforcement and community safety incidents lead to insurance claims or legal proceedings that require investigation beyond the CivicMesh evidence package. Having PI capability in the Guard Cooperative creates a full-service response capability that no competitor can offer. |
