| The Ottawa pilot is coming | CivicMesh's OC Transpo pilot will generate demand for certified technicians the moment it goes live. Companies with pre-certified staff win contracts. Companies that start certifying after the pilot launches are months behind. |
|---|---|
| Differentiation in RFPs | Ottawa is already preparing for CivicMesh deployments. Procurement officers will ask prospective vendors: 'Do you have CivicMesh-certified staff?' Certified = shortlisted. Not certified = long conversation. |

# 3. The Algonquin College Partnership
Algonquin College is the first accredited CivicMesh Training Provider in Canada. Their Continuing Education programme offers CivicMesh Node Technician certification as a recognized certificate programme. Ottawa security companies who want their staff certified through an accredited college — for recruitment, retention, and professional development purposes — can enroll directly through Algonquin's CE division.
