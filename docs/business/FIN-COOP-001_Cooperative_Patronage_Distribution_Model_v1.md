# 1. CCSC Patronage Distribution Formula


| Revenue Source | CCSC Receives | Members Receive (80%) | CCSC Retains (20%) |
|---|---|---|---|
| TrafficMesh citation revenue share | Per commercial agreement — 15% of gross citations from civilian/community nodes | 80% distributed to contributing node owners pro-rata by attributed citations | 20% to CCSC operating fund and reserve |
| CommunityShield fine revenue share | 25% of community fines from CCSC-member nodes | 70% to contributing node owners; 10% pro-rata to all active members | 20% to CCSC fund |
| Government node owner stipends | 100% pass-through — municipality pays stipend through CCSC as administrator | 100% — CCSC retains nothing on stipend pass-through | 0% |
| Insurance rebates | 100% pass-through — insurer pays directly to opted-in member accounts | 100% — individual negotiation between member and insurer | 0% |
| Data marketplace allocation | Per data product revenue share agreement with CivicMesh Inc. | 60% to active node owners pro-rata by node uptime hours | 40% to CCSC fund |


# 2. Patronage Payout Options and Tax Treatment


| Option | Description | Tax Treatment | When Available |
|---|---|---|---|
| CAD Direct Deposit | Patronage amount transferred to member's registered bank account. | Taxable income in year received. CCSC issues T4A for amounts >$500/year. | Monthly for amounts >$50. Annual for smaller amounts (December). |
| D-Credit | Patronage converted to D-Credit at par (1 D-Credit = $1 CAD). Spendable within D-Central ecosystem at 5% bonus rate. | Tax position: D-Credit received as patronage should be taxable on receipt at par value. Consult tax counsel before large D-Credit elections — CRA has not issued specific guidance on cooperative digital currencies. | Monthly for all amounts. |
| Platform Fee Credit | Patronage applied as credit against CCSC membership fee, CivicMesh platform fees, or training certification fees. | Reduces taxable patronage — credit reduces a deductible expense. Net tax effect: tax neutral vs. receiving CAD and paying the fee separately. | Monthly — applied before next fee cycle. |
| Transit Credit | Patronage donated as OC Transpo Presto credit (where municipal agreement active). | Charitable donation equivalent? — legal opinion required before offering this option. CRA position unclear. | Monthly — if municipal agreement active. |


# 3. Annual Distribution Schedule


| Activity | Timing | Responsible |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly patronage calculation | 1st of each month for prior month | CivicMesh platform revenue accounting system |
| Monthly D-Credit and fee credit distributions | 5th of each month | Automated via Member App |
| Monthly CAD distributions (>$50) | 10th of each month | CCSC treasury — direct deposit batch |
| Annual CAD distribution (annual amounts <$50/month accumulated) | December 15 | CCSC treasury — annual batch |
| T4A slip generation (patronage >$500) | January 31 for prior year | CCSC accounting — distributed via Member App and mailed |
| Annual patronage statement (all members) | February 15 for prior year | Member App — downloadable PDF |
| CCSC AGM — patronage summary presented to members | Within 6 months of fiscal year end (by June 30) | CCSC board + Manager Dashboard report generation |


# 4. D-Credit Velocity Incentive


| What it is | Members who spend D-Credit within the D-Central ecosystem (on platform fees, training certifications, cooperative membership fees, D-Central partner merchant purchases) receive a 5% velocity bonus on the amount spent. |
|---|---|
| Example | Member earns $100 in patronage → elects D-Credit → receives 100 D-Credit (= $100 value). Member spends 50 D-Credit on L1 technician certification renewal. Velocity bonus: 2.5 D-Credit (5% of 50). Member's D-Credit balance: 52.5 D-Credit remaining. |
| Economic effect | The velocity incentive creates a local circular economy: patronage earned from community safety contributions is spent within the cooperative ecosystem, generating more activity, which generates more patronage. This is the cooperative economic flywheel D-Credit is designed to create. |
| Tax note | The 5% velocity bonus is additional patronage — taxable as income in the year the bonus D-Credit is issued. For small amounts this is de minimis — at scale, members should be advised to track D-Credit bonuses for T4A purposes. |
