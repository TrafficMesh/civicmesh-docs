# Design Brief


| Slide count | 15 slides. Council presentations have strict time limits (typically 10–15 minutes) — every slide must earn its place. |
|---|---|
| Department value slides | Slides 5–10 cover Public Works, OC Transpo, Ottawa Police, Urban Planning, Emergency Management, and Bylaw Services. Each gets one slide. Use icons and dollar figures — councillors respond to quantified value. |
| Revenue gap visual | Slide 2: large-format bar chart showing $38.7M gap with TrafficMesh projection overlay. This is the hook — it needs to be visually striking. |
| Digital twin demo | If a live digital twin prototype is available for Ottawa routes, a 30-second screen recording embedded in slide 8 is more compelling than a static screenshot. |
| Political defence slide | Slide 12: the objection matrix should be formatted as a simple table — question on left, one-sentence answer on right. Councillors will be looking for the answers to questions their constituents will ask. |
