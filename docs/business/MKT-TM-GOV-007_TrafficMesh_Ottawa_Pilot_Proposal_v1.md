# 1. Proposal Summary


| Executive Summary
TrafficMesh Technologies Inc. proposes a 6-month, 30-bus pilot deployment of the TrafficMesh mobile enforcement platform on designated OC Transpo bus routes. The pilot will generate enforcement citations for bus lane and bus stop obstruction violations, operate under existing HTA authority with mandatory officer review before every citation, and generate an estimated $225,000–$900,000 in gross citation revenue to be shared between OC Transpo and the City of Ottawa per the proposed revenue share agreement. The pilot requires no capital expenditure by OC Transpo beyond fleet access for installation. |
|---|


# 2. Pilot Scope and Terms


| Duration | 6 months from first node activation |
|---|---|
| Fleet enrolled | 30 OC Transpo buses — mix of articulated and standard — on Transitway, Bank Street, and Albert/Slater corridors |
| Violation types | Phase 1: Bus stop obstruction (HTA s.170(15)) and bus lane obstruction (municipal bus lane bylaw). Speed: advisory only in Phase 1. |
| Officer review | City of Ottawa Bylaw Services provides 2 trained officers (Track 5 certified) for evidence queue review. Target response time: 4 hours for Fast-Track queue. |
| Hardware | Provided by TrafficMesh Technologies Inc. at no capital cost to OC Transpo. Hardware remains property of TrafficMesh. Removed at end of pilot or on expiry of commercial agreement. |
| Installation | Certified L2 technicians. Fleet garage access 02:00–06:00. Approximately 6 buses per installation night. Zero disruption to bus operations. |
| MSSP Services | D-Central Shield operates NOC 24/7 during pilot. SLA: 99.5% evidence pipeline uptime, 4-hour P1 incident response. |
| Monthly reporting | TrafficMesh delivers monthly KPI report to OC Transpo VP Operations: citation volume, revenue generated, system uptime, dismissal rate, any contested citations. |


# 3. Revenue Share Framework


| Party | Share | Basis | Payment Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| City of Ottawa (enforcing authority) | 65% of net citation revenue | Net = gross citation amount minus provincial administration fee | Monthly — TrafficMesh processes and remits within 30 days of citation collection |
| TrafficMesh Technologies / D-Central Shield (MSSP platform fee) | 20% of net citation revenue | Platform fee covering: NOC operations, evidence pipeline, officer portal, MSSP services | Monthly — retained before remittance to city |
| OC Transpo Reinvestment Fund | 5% of net citation revenue | Direct allocation to OC Transpo service improvement fund — documented separately from city general revenue | Monthly — to dedicated OC Transpo account |
| CCSC Community Fund | 10% of net citation revenue | Seed funding for civilian opt-in programme launch in Phase 3 | Monthly — to CCSC account |
