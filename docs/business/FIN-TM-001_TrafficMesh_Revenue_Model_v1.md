# 2. Phase 1 — OC Transpo Pilot Revenue Detail


| Citation volume projection | Based on NYC MTA ACE data: 30 buses on high-density corridors generate approximately 50–200 validated citations per day across the fleet. At $150 average fine: $7,500–$30,000 gross citation revenue per day. Monthly: $225,000–$900,000. |
|---|---|
| TrafficMesh MSSP revenue (20%) | $45,000–$180,000 per month gross citation revenue × 20% = $9,000–$36,000/month. Minus operating costs (~$4,500/month for 30 nodes): net $4,500–$31,500/month. |
| NOC management fees | $450/month (30 nodes × $15/node). Modest but recurring. |
| Conservative projection for planning | Use $5,000–$15,000/month net MSSP revenue from Phase 1 pilot for financial planning. Do not plan on upper bound — use 30th percentile for cash flow. |
| Timing | Revenue begins approximately Month 8 (after 6-month software MVP + 2-month installation and soft launch). First full revenue month: approximately Month 10. |
