# 1. The Property Manager Value Proposition


| Problem | CommunityShield Solution | Time Saved Per Month |
|---|---|---|
| Parking enforcement requires staff to physically document violations | CommunityShield captures violations passively. Manager reviews clips and clicks confirm. No physical documentation. | 4–8 hours/month |
| Residents complain about safety incidents — no documentation | Every incident captured with timestamp, GPS, and clip. Manager can review and respond with evidence. | 2–4 hours/month on incident disputes |
| Insurance premium reviews require documentation of security measures | Annual coverage density report auto-generated by platform. Submitted to insurer directly. | 3–5 hours/year |
| Annual general meeting prep requires security summary | Transparency report auto-generated from audit log. AGM slide deck auto-generated from report. | 4–8 hours/year |
| Bylaw enforcement requires resident complaints before action | Passive detection means violations are captured regardless of whether a resident reports them. | 1–3 hours/month on complaint intake |


# 2. The Resident Consent Workflow — What to Tell the Board


| What the board needs to know | 'The Condominium Act requires notice to owners before installing cameras. We handle the notice template and the 30-day notice period. You distribute it. We activate after the period expires.' |
|---|---|
| What residents are told | 'Your building is deploying CommunityShield. Here is where every camera will be. Here is what it captures. Here is how to see your data. Here is how to opt in to the revenue share programme. Here is how to opt out of everything.' |
| What residents get | Residents who opt in their own doorbell cameras to the network earn a monthly patronage payment proportional to citations attributed to their device. Average: $20–$60/month for active Ottawa community. |
| What the board approves | A board resolution authorizing CommunityShield deployment under the Condominium Act. Template resolution provided. Legal counsel review recommended before vote. |
