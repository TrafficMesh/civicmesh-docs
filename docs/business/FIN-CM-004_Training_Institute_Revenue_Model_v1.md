# 3. Algonquin Partnership Economics


| Year 1 — D-Central as sole provider | D-Central Training Institute delivers all certifications. 100% of the 60% provider share flows to D-Central. Establishes the programme, builds the curriculum, validates the assessment framework. |
|---|---|
| Year 2 — Algonquin onboarded | Algonquin Continuing Education delivers Track 1 L1 and L2 to their security integrator network. Estimated 250 certifications/year at $299/$499 average: ~$98,750 gross × 60% = $59,250 to Algonquin. |
| Algonquin's ROI | $59,250 revenue in Year 2 for a programme that requires: instructor training (D-Central provides), LMS setup (existing Brightspace infrastructure), curriculum materials (provided by CTC). Marginal cost to Algonquin is minimal. ROI is very high. |
| Mitacs leverage | Algonquin as accredited CTC training provider + Algonquin as Mitacs Accelerate Entrepreneur incubator = two simultaneous Algonquin relationships. Both are referenced in the Mitacs application. Combined, they make the application significantly more competitive. |
