# Presentation Script — Key Slides


| Slide | Content | Design Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Cover | [Community name]'s Safety Network — CommunityShield by CivicMesh | Use community's logo if permission granted. Personal touch matters for HOA pitches. |
| 2 — Your Problem | Specific parking violation count (if obtainable). Recent security incidents. The cost of manual enforcement. | Use real numbers from this community if available. Generic stats are less compelling. |
| 3 — The Solution | On-device blurring diagram. Evidence-triggered-only timeline. Local storage explanation. | The privacy slide must come early — before the revenue slide. Board members will not proceed past their privacy concerns. |
| 4 — Revenue | Fine revenue distribution pie chart: 60% HOA, 25% node owners, 15% platform. | Make the HOA's 60% visually dominant. Then show the node owner share as the resident incentive. |
| 5 — Insurance | Before/after premium comparison with certification (illustrative, not guaranteed). | 'We can't guarantee a specific reduction — but here is what comparable communities have achieved.' |
| 6–10 | Installation, Manager Dashboard, privacy rights, camera map, next steps | Keep final 5 slides simple and operational — the decision slides are 1–5. |
