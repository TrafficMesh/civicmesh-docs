# 1. Slide-by-Slide Narrative


| Slide | Title | Key Message | Speaker Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Your Community Has a Safety Problem We Can Solve | Parking violations, access violations, package theft — costs you money and time to enforce. | Open with the specific problems this community faces. Research before the meeting: how many parking violations per month? Has there been recent vandalism or trespassing? |
| 2 | CommunityShield — Community-Owned Safety Infrastructure | Not Ring. Not Flock. Owned by your community, run for your community, revenue back to your community. | Stress: your HOA owns the data. The footage never goes to Amazon or any other cloud company without your manager's explicit action. |
| 3 | What It Captures | Event-triggered clips only. No continuous recording. Faces blurred on-device before anything leaves the camera. Your residents' privacy is architecturally protected. | Show the on-device blurring diagram. 'The camera blurs faces before any footage leaves the device. There is no technical way for us to see unblurred footage of your residents.' |
| 4 | What Your Community Earns | Parking fine revenue: 60% to HOA operating fund. Node owner residents earn 25%. Platform processing fee: 15%. | Make it concrete: 'If your parking lot generates 10 violations per month at $75 each: $750 gross. Your HOA keeps $450. Residents with dashcams earn the rest.' |
| 5 | Insurance Premium Reduction | After 90 days of operation, we issue a coverage density certificate to your insurer. Expect 5–15% premium reduction on your commercial property insurance. | Give a dollar amount if you know their premium. '5% on a $20,000 premium is $1,000 back annually.' |
| 6 | The Installation Process | Certified technicians. One day on-site. Cameras at entrances and parking areas. NAS in management office. You're live within 10 business days of signing. | Address concerns about disruption. 'Your residents won't know we were there.' |
| 7 | What Your Manager Sees | Manager Dashboard: active nodes, incident queue, revenue, annual report. 10 minutes a day to manage. | Show the Manager Dashboard screenshot. It should look simple and unintimidating. |
| 8 | Resident Privacy Rights | Any resident can see where every camera is. Any resident can request their data. Any resident can opt out. Law enforcement can only access footage through a formal legal request — and your manager is notified within 48 hours. | This is the slide that addresses board member concerns about 'turning the condo into a surveillance state.' |
| 9 | What It Costs | Monthly platform fee: $[TBD] for your community size. Installation: $[TBD]. Payback: [X] months based on your enforcement revenue projection. | Have the numbers ready for this specific community before you walk in. |
| 10 | Recommended Next Steps | Pilot with 2 cameras for 90 days. No long-term commitment. Cancel anytime. | Make the ask as small as possible. 'Try 2 cameras for 3 months. If you don't like it, we remove them at no charge.' |
