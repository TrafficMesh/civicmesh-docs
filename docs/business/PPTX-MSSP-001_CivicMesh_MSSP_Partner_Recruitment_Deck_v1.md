# Design Brief


| Opening | Lead with the revenue opportunity, not the technology. '$38M revenue gap in Ottawa alone. 444 Ontario municipalities with the same problem. No certified MSSP in the market yet. That is your opportunity.' |
|---|---|
| The revenue model slide | Show the MSSP unit economics clearly: $18–$88/node/month gross, break-even at 100 nodes, margin at scale. Investors and prospective MSSPs respond to clear economics. |
| The backend slide | 'Here is what D-Central provides so you don't have to.' List: platform infrastructure, AI models, privacy compliance, legal framework, officer portal. Show this as a visual — what D-Central provides on the left, what the MSSP provides on the right. The MSSP side should look manageable. |
| Reference client | If OC Transpo pilot is active: 'Ottawa is your reference market.' One slide, one compelling client, real data. |
