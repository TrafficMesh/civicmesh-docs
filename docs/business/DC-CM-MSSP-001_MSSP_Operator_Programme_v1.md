| Executive Summary
The CivicMesh MSSP Operator Programme defines the service catalog, SLA framework, operational requirements, and certification path for MSSP operators. D-Central Shield Inc. is the founding MSSP operator and the operational template from which all external MSSP certifications are derived. The programme is designed to allow D-Central Shield to hand off clients to certified external MSSPs as geographic coverage expands, while maintaining platform quality standards. |

| Tier | Requirements | Revenue Sharing | Territory |

# 3. D-Central Shield — Founding MSSP


| Role | D-Central Shield operates as MSSP for all D-Central-direct clients (OC Transpo pilot, first HOA deployments) and as the operational proof of concept from which the MSSP programme standards are derived. |
|---|---|
| Transition Plan | As external MSSPs are certified, D-Central Shield transitions clients in mature markets to external MSSPs and focuses on: (a) highest-complexity or highest-sensitivity clients, (b) new market entry (first client in a new city), (c) MSSP certification oversight and auditing. |
| Conflict Management | D-Central Shield competes with external MSSPs on equal terms within the MSSP Cooperative governance structure. D-Central retains a permanent founding member vote in the MSSP Cooperative to prevent hostile governance by any single large MSSP. |
