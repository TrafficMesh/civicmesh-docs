# 1. Certification Tier Requirements


| Requirement | Bronze | Silver | Gold |
|---|---|---|---|
| Certified MSSP Operator Track staff | Minimum 2 | Minimum 4 | Minimum 8 (dedicated CivicMesh team) |
| NOC staffing | Business hours (8am–8pm M–F) | Extended hours (6am–10pm 7 days) | 24/7 dedicated NOC |
| Security certification | Annual pen test by approved firm | SOC 2 Type II equivalent | SOC 2 Type II + annual D-Central audit |
| E&O insurance | $2M CAD | $5M CAD | $10M CAD |
| Cyber liability insurance | $2M CAD | $5M CAD | $10M CAD |
| Platform uptime SLA | 99.5% | 99.9% | 99.95% |
| P1 incident response | 4 hours | 1 hour | 30 minutes |
| MSSP Cooperative membership | Required | Required | Required — Gold membership |
| Annual audit | Self-assessment | D-Central desk review | D-Central on-site audit |


# 2. Certification Process
- MSSP submits application with: company profile, insurance certificates, security certification evidence, staff credential inventory, technical infrastructure overview
- D-Central reviews documentation — 15 business days
- For Silver/Gold: D-Central conducts technical infrastructure assessment (remote or on-site)
- Certification decision communicated with detailed findings
- On approval: MSSPOperatorCredential issued to MSSP DID, on-chain registration, MSSP Partner Agreement (LEGAL-CM-010) executed
- Annual renewal: submit updated documentation → abbreviated desk review → credential renewed
# 3. Ongoing Compliance Monitoring


| Automated Monitoring | D-Central NOC monitors all certified MSSP deployments: uptime vs SLA, incident response times, evidence pipeline integrity, federation protocol compliance. Automatic alerts on SLA breach. |
|---|---|
| Quarterly Review | D-Central reviews MSSP performance report quarterly. Persistent SLA breaches trigger corrective action plan. |
| Credential Suspension | Immediate credential suspension triggers: security breach affecting community data, unauthorized data sharing, AGPL licence violation, loss of required insurance. Suspension logged on-chain within 24 hours. |
