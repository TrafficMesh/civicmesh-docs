# Design Brief


| Colour palette | Dark Blue #1F3864 (headers), Mid Blue #2E75B6 (accent), White (background). OC Transpo brand colours may be incorporated in slide 1 cover. |
|---|---|
| Slide count | 12 slides. Maximum. Every slide that can be cut should be cut. |
| Data visualizations required | Slide 2: bus corridor speed data (bar chart). Slide 7: revenue model pie chart (city/platform/CCSC/OC Transpo split). Slide 10: installation timeline Gantt. |
| Photos required | Slide 4: officer review portal screenshot (real Ottawa footage from test deployment). Slide 5: NYC MTA bus with camera visible. Slide 9: OC Transpo bus (cleared for commercial use). |
| Key design principle | This is a government procurement presentation — it should look professional and credible, not startup-flashy. Clean, data-forward, no animations except simple slide transitions. |
| Demo requirement | Slide 4 may be replaced with a live demo of the officer review portal if laptop is available at the meeting. Prepare both options. |
