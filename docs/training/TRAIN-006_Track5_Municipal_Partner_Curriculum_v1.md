# 1. Curriculum Modules (6 hours, $599)


| Module | Content | Duration | Assessment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Module A — TrafficMesh Evidence Overview | How evidence packages are generated. What's in an evidence package. The credential chain and what it proves. The officer's role in the legal chain. | 60 min | Written exam (15 questions, 75% pass) |
| Module B — Officer Portal Operation | Portal navigation. Queue types (Fast-Track, Standard, Manual). Viewing evidence clips. The minimum review standard for each queue type. DID signature and what it means legally. | 90 min | Practical — supervised portal session with 10 test evidence packages |
| Module C — Dismissal Discipline | Dismissal reason codes and when to use each. Why accurate dismissal codes matter (model retraining). The cost of incorrect dismissal codes (under-enforcement and over-enforcement). | 45 min | Case study — 5 dismissal decision scenarios |
| Module D — Citation Workflow | From confirmation to citation issuance. MTO integration. Provincial Offence Notice requirements. Revenue attribution. Record-keeping obligations. | 45 min | Written exam (10 questions) |
| Module E — Contested Citation Preparation | What happens when a citation is contested. Generating the legal evidence package. What defence counsel will challenge. Expert witness support. Officer's role at a hearing. | 60 min | Case study — simulated contested citation preparation |
| Module F — HTA Compliance and Legal Framework | Overview of REG-TM-001 key findings. What TrafficMesh evidence is and is not. Provincial Offences Act context. How to answer questions from the public about the programme. | 30 min | Written exam (10 questions) |


# 2. Ongoing Officer Certification Obligations


| Obligation | Frequency | Consequence of Non-Compliance |
|---|---|---|
| Annual renewal — refresher module | Annual — 2-hour online refresher + 20-question exam | Officer Authorization Credential expires. No further citations can be issued until renewal complete. |
| Background recheck | Annual — updated CPIC check | Credential revoked if disqualifying finding |
| Dismissal rate review | Quarterly — supervisor reviews officer's dismissal code patterns | Persistent anomalies (unusually high dismissal rate or unusually low dismissal rate) trigger supervisor review and potential retraining |
| New violation type certification | When new violation types are activated in the officer's jurisdiction | Separate module certification required for each new violation type added to the officer's authorized set |
