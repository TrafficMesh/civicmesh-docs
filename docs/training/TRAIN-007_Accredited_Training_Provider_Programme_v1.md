# 2. Accreditation Requirements


| Requirement | Standard | Verification |
|---|---|---|
| Instructor qualifications | Technical tracks (1, 2): instructors must hold the certification level they teach + 1 level above. Non-technical tracks: relevant domain expertise. | Credential verification at accreditation application |
| Delivery infrastructure | LMS capable of: video delivery, embedded quizzes, assessment submission, automated tracking. Proctoring capability for L2/L3 practical assessments. | Technical review of LMS and proctoring approach |
| Assessment integrity | Written assessments: administered through approved LMS with plagiarism detection. Practical assessments: in-person proctored or remote proctored via approved platform. | Sample assessment review at accreditation + annual spot audit |
| Curriculum compliance | Deliver curriculum as specified in TRAIN-002 through TRAIN-006. Additions permitted. Omissions are not. | Annual curriculum audit by CTC Standards Committee |
| Record retention | Assessment records retained minimum 5 years. Available to CTC within 10 business days of audit request. | Annual audit right |
| Cooperative membership | Provider joins CivicMesh Training Cooperative as a member provider. Annual membership fee: $2,000. | CTC membership application |


# 3. Accreditation Process
- Provider submits accreditation application to CTC: institutional profile, intended tracks, instructor credentials, LMS evidence, assessment samples.
- CTC Standards Committee review within 30 business days.
- Technical review of delivery infrastructure — remote review or site visit for high-volume providers.
- Approval decision communicated with conditions if any.
- Provider signs CTC Provider Agreement. Joins CTC as member provider.
- Provider DID registered in CivicMesh Trust Registry as authorized issuer for approved certification tracks.
- First cohort delivered under CTC supervision — sample assessments reviewed.
- Annual renewal: abbreviated review + curriculum compliance confirmation + revenue reconciliation.
