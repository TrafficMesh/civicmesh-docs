# 1. Curriculum Modules (4 hours, $399)


| Module | Content | Duration | Assessment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Module A — Platform Overview for Managers | What CivicMesh does (not technical — operational). What the Manager Dashboard shows. What incidents look like. What revenue looks like. What the cooperative membership means for your community. | 60 min | Embedded quiz (10 questions) |
| Module B — Incident Queue Management | How to review an incident clip. Confirm vs. dismiss decision framework. Violation types and evidence standards for each. How to issue community notices. When to dispatch security response. | 60 min | Scenario assessment — 5 incident review scenarios |
| Module C — Law Enforcement Request Handling | The formal request process. What to do if police contact you directly (redirect to portal). How to read the federation log. Community notification obligations. When to contact your MSSP. | 45 min | Case study — simulated law enforcement contact scenario |
| Module D — Member and Privacy Management | Resident onboarding and opt-in. Data access requests. Deletion requests. Privacy complaints. Annual transparency report review and publication. AGM preparation. | 45 min | Written exam (10 questions) |
| Module E — Cooperative Governance | What it means to be a cooperative manager. Member rights. Patronage distribution. Your role in the cooperative AGM. How community members can raise concerns. | 30 min | Embedded quiz (5 questions) |
