| Executive Summary
FedDev Ontario supports economic development, business growth, and innovation in Southern Ontario. D-Central Group is an Ottawa-based technology company creating cooperative employment and community economic infrastructure — a strong fit for FedDev's mandate. FedDev programme openings are not constant — they open and close based on federal budget cycles and government priorities. This document is a readiness framework: the narrative, financial data, and supporting evidence needed to respond quickly when a relevant FedDev programme opens. |
|---|


# 1. D-Central FedDev Value Proposition


| Regional Economic Impact | D-Central Group creates cooperative employment in Ottawa — technicians (certified through CivicMesh Training Institute), MSSP operators, security response providers, and NOC/SOC staff. The cooperative model ensures that economic value generated by the platform stays in the Ottawa region rather than flowing to out-of-province shareholders. |
|---|---|
| Technology Leadership | CivicMesh is globally differentiated technology — privacy-preserving, open-source, community-owned enforcement infrastructure with no comparable product in Canada or internationally. Ottawa-based development of this platform builds regional technology leadership in an emerging sector. |
| Export Potential | Once the Ottawa pilot is proven, CivicMesh is exportable to every Canadian municipality, then to the US and international markets. FedDev investment in the Ottawa development phase leverages significant future export revenue. |
| Community Economic Infrastructure | The cooperative structure means FedDev's investment builds community-owned infrastructure, not extractive private capital. This aligns with FedDev's community economic development mandate. |
| Jobs Created | Projected employment from CivicMesh Ottawa deployment: [15–25] direct jobs (D-Central staff) + [50–100] indirect jobs (certified technicians, MSSP operators, security response providers, training providers) within 3 years of platform launch. |
