# CivicMesh Documentation

Welcome to the **CivicMesh** documentation. This is the central hub for all technical, business, and operational information about the distributed traffic enforcement and city intelligence platform.

## What is CivicMesh?

**CivicMesh** is a distributed platform combining:
- **Fleet vehicles** (OC Transpo buses, city vehicles, contractors) with on-device AI
- **Civilian opt-in crowdsourcing** via consumer hardware kits
- **Real-time violation detection** with human-reviewed enforcement
- **Digital twin of city road infrastructure** for intelligent city planning
- **Multi-tiered business model**: open source core + MSSP + managed IT

### Vision
Transform traffic enforcement and city intelligence through distributed AI, transparency, and community participation.

### Success Metrics (Year 1)
- 50+ buses deployed in OC Transpo pilot
- $100K/month revenue
- <5% false positive rate
- 5% reduction in bus lane collisions
