| Executive Summary
TrafficMesh Technologies Inc. is the operating entity for all TrafficMesh deployments. It contracts directly with municipalities, OC Transpo, and fleet operators. It employs the technical team building the TrafficMesh platform. It files SR&ED claims for TrafficMesh-specific R&D. It is a CCPC, 100% owned by D-Central Group Inc., receiving its IP rights through an intercompany sublicence from CivicMesh Inc. This entity is the one that signs the OC Transpo pilot agreement. |
