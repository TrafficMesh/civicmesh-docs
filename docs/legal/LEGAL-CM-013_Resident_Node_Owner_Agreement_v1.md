| Executive Summary
This is the individual consumer-facing agreement residents sign when connecting a personal device to the CivicMesh network as a node. It must be written in plain language (Ontario plain language standard — grade 8 reading level), clearly explain what data is and is not collected, be fully PIPEDA-compliant, and make the opt-in and opt-out process unambiguous. This document will be scrutinized by privacy advocates and media — it must be impeccable and honest. |
|---|




| What you're agreeing to — plain language summary (displayed before full agreement)
• YOU GET: A share of enforcement revenue from violations your device helps detect. A cooperative membership in CCSC. An insurance premium reduction if you opt into the driving data share. A vote in how your community's safety network is governed.
• WE COLLECT: Short video clips when your device detects a potential violation (not continuous footage). GPS location of your device. Timestamps. License plates in clips (blurred before leaving your device for non-enforcement clips).
• WE DO NOT COLLECT: Continuous video footage. Your identity from the footage. Your driving route for advertising. Any data you haven't consented to share.
• LAW ENFORCEMENT ACCESS: Only on a formal legal request. Every request is logged and you will be notified within 48 hours of any access to your data.
• YOU CAN LEAVE ANYTIME: Disconnect your device, request deletion of all your data, and cancel your membership at any time. No penalty. |

# 1. Consent and Opt-In Structure


| Data Category | Consent Type | Default | Changeable? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Violation detection clip generation | Implied — core service. Cannot opt out and remain a node owner. | On | No — core function |
| TrafficMesh enforcement contribution (public road violations) | Explicit opt-in at onboarding | Off | Yes — toggle in Member App anytime |
| CommunityShield community camera contribution | Explicit opt-in at onboarding | Off | Yes — toggle in Member App |
| MeshPlate ALPR contribution | Explicit opt-in at onboarding | Off | Yes — toggle in Member App |
| Insurance driving record share (own vehicle only) | Explicit opt-in + insurer selection | Off | Yes — separate opt-in per insurer |
| MeshNav anonymous movement contribution | Explicit opt-in at onboarding | Off | Yes — toggle in Member App |
| Aggregate data for research (anonymised — no PII) | Implied with cooperative membership | On | Yes — can opt out |


# 2. Your Data Rights


| Access | Request a complete copy of all data held about you at any time: Member App → Settings → Privacy → Download My Data. Fulfilled within 30 days. |
|---|---|
| Correction | Contact privacy@civicmesh.ca to correct any inaccurate data. Corrections fulfilled within 30 days. |
| Deletion | Request deletion of all your data at any time. Deleted within 30 days, except: data in active law enforcement investigations (deleted when investigation closes) and data required by law. |
| Portability | Your cooperative membership credential (Verifiable Credential) is held in your own DID wallet — it is portable and does not disappear if you leave the network. |
| Opt-Out | Withdraw any consent at any time by toggling the relevant setting in the Member App. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal. |


# 3. Compensation Terms


| Patronage Basis | You receive patronage from CCSC based on validated enforcement citations attributed to your node(s). Not guaranteed — depends on your node's coverage area and violation volume. |
|---|---|
| Payment Methods | CAD direct deposit, D-Credit, platform fee credit, or transit credit (where available). Selected in Member App. |
| Tax Obligations | Patronage payments are income. CCSC will issue a T4A slip for annual patronage exceeding $500 CAD. You are responsible for reporting this income on your tax return. |
| Government Stipends | If your municipality operates a node owner incentive programme, your stipend is paid by the municipality through CCSC as administrator. |
| Insurance Rebates | Insurance premium reductions are between you and your insurer. CivicMesh facilitates the data sharing but does not guarantee any specific reduction amount. |
