| Executive Summary
Trademark registration protects the D-Central brand family against third-party use. Filing immediately after incorporation — before public launch — establishes the earliest possible priority date. The Canadian filing starts the 12-month Paris Convention window for US and international applications. A cleared NUANS name search (required for incorporation) does not guarantee trademark clearance — separate trademark searches are required for each mark. |
|---|
