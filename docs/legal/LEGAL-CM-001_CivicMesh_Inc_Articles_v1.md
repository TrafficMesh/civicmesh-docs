| Executive Summary
CivicMesh Inc. is the primary operating subsidiary for the CivicMesh platform. It holds CivicMesh-specific IP (licenced from D-Central Group Inc.), issues MSSP franchise agreements, governs the open source dual-licence structure, and operates the data marketplace and insurance API. It is a wholly-owned CCPC subsidiary, ensuring SR&ED eligibility for platform R&D conducted within the entity. |
|---|


# 1. Entity Scope and Revenue Streams


| Platform IP Held | CivicMesh platform architecture, federation protocol, privacy architecture, DID/VC schema registry, NOC dashboard, all five module codebases |
|---|---|
| Revenue Streams | MSSP platform fees, data marketplace subscriptions (insurance API, municipal data), open source commercial licences, training cooperative revenue share |
| Does NOT Operate | Direct municipal enforcement (TrafficMesh Technologies Inc.), community camera deployments (CommunityShield Inc.), or security response (D-Central Guard Inc.) |
| Governance | Wholly owned by D-Central Group Inc. Mission lock provisions mirror LEGAL-002. |


# 2. SR&ED Activities
- Federation protocol design — novel privacy-preserving upward data federation architecture
- DID/VC credential schema development — W3C standard implementation for multi-entity ecosystem
- On-chain anchoring — Polygon-based credential registry and revocation system (StatusList2021)
- Privacy architecture — hash-before-store ALPR, on-device face blurring pipeline, consent management engine
- Insurance data product development — novel actuarial data pipeline from community safety infrastructure
