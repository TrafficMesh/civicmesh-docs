| Executive Summary
The CivicMesh Security Response Cooperative governs the certified security response providers who receive dispatch through the CivicMesh platform. D-Central Guard Inc. is the founding member and first certified responder. As other licensed security companies become certified CivicMesh responders, they join the cooperative. Members receive patronage from dispatch revenue, set response standards collectively, and participate in the continuous improvement of the dispatch protocol. This cooperative structure converts competing security companies into network participants with shared economic interests in the platform's growth. |
|---|


# 1. Membership and Dispatch Revenue Share


| Membership Eligibility | Licensed security provider in Ontario (Private Security and Investigative Services Act), CivicMesh Response Track 4 certified, valid E&O and liability insurance, active dispatch integration |
|---|---|
| Membership Fee | $2,500 CAD per year |
| Dispatch Revenue Model | Per-dispatch fee paid by community (HOA, campus, municipality) to CivicMesh platform. Platform retains [X]%, dispatches [Y]% to responding provider. Cooperative patronage returns [Z]% of platform's dispatch revenue share to all members pro-rata by dispatch volume. |
| Response SLA | Standard: dispatch acceptance within 5 minutes; on-scene within 30 minutes (urban Ottawa). Gold: 3 minutes / 20 minutes. SLA tier determines dispatch queue priority. |


# 2. D-Central Guard — First Responder


| Role | D-Central Guard Inc. is the first certified response provider and serves as the operational proof of concept for the dispatch model before other providers are certified. |
|---|---|
| Founding Member | Holds one permanent cooperative founding member vote. Sets initial response standards that become the cooperative standard. |
| Transition Plan | As cooperative membership grows, D-Central Guard transitions from default responder to one competitive provider among many — competing on response time and quality, not on exclusivity. |
| Geographic Priority | D-Central Guard focuses on Ottawa central while certifying partner providers for suburbs, other Ontario cities, and eventually other provinces. |


# 3. Integration with Private Investigation


| PI Services | Cooperative members who hold private investigation licences (in addition to security licences) may receive referrals for evidence package follow-up investigations from D-Central Forensics. |
|---|---|
| Cost Reduction Argument | Municipal client pitch: CivicMesh + cooperative response dispatch reduces sworn officer dispatch costs by routing sub-criminal incidents to licensed security response at [30–50]% lower cost per incident. Police called only when cooperative responder confirms escalation need. |
| Chain of Custody | All cooperative responder actions are logged with responder DID signature, timestamped, and attached to the originating evidence package chain of custody. |
