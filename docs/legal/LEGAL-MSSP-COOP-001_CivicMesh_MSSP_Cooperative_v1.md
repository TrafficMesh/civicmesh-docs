| Executive Summary
The CivicMesh MSSP Cooperative gives certified MSSP operators a formal cooperative stake in the platform standards and governance. Members participate in setting platform SLAs, certification requirements, and service catalog standards. Patronage returns flow from platform fee revenue to members proportional to their nodes under management. This converts channel partners from commercial licensees into cooperative stakeholders — with aligned incentives and governance rights that increase switching costs significantly. |
|---|


# 1. Membership and Governance


| Membership Eligibility | Any CivicMesh-certified MSSP Gold operator in good standing (current certification, SLA compliance, no outstanding audit findings) |
|---|---|
| Membership Fee | $5,000 CAD per year |
| Voting | One member one vote. D-Central Group Inc. holds one founding member vote (permanent, non-dilutive). |
| Platform Standards Committee | Elected from membership. Reviews and approves SLA changes, service catalog additions, certification requirement changes. D-Central retains veto on security and privacy standards changes. |
| Patronage Return | [X]% of net platform fee revenue (after D-Central operating costs) distributed to members pro-rata by nodes under management in the prior year. |


# 2. What Members Gain Beyond Commercial Access


| Benefit | Description |
|---|---|
| Patronage returns | Pro-rata share of platform fee revenue — MSSP operators earn from the platform's total success, not just their own clients |
| Governance rights | Vote on platform standards, SLA minimums, certification requirements — the standards you must meet are standards you help set |
| Early feature access | MSSP Cooperative members get early access to new platform features for client pilot testing |
| Collective marketing | MSSP Cooperative brand badge for use in municipal procurement bids — signals D-Central ecosystem alignment and cooperative values |
| Peer network | Annual MSSP Cooperative conference, shared playbooks, shared sales tools — competing MSSPs on geography, cooperating on platform improvement |


# 3. D-Central's Role as Founding Member


| Founding Member Status | D-Central Group Inc. holds one permanent founding member vote. This prevents hostile takeover of platform standards by a single large MSSP operator. |
|---|---|
| Technical Authority | D-Central retains unilateral authority over: security architecture changes, privacy architecture changes, open source licence terms, DID/VC schema updates. All other platform standards subject to cooperative governance. |
| Platform Operator | D-Central Shield Inc. (D-Central's own MSSP) is also a Cooperative member, competing with other MSSPs on equal governance terms. |
